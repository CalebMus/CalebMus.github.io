About Caleb Müsfeldt

General / Research

I am a data scientist with a focus on artificial intelligence and cognitive science. I am driven by a deep passion for developing AI that can think, reason, and learn in ways that reflect human intelligence. My research experience spans applied math, algorithm design, neuroscience, cognitive science, and LLMs.

I plan to start a PhD program in 2025 where my goal is to develop neural network architectures with the capabilities of causal reasoning, out-of-distribution generalizations, and intuitive physics. My career will be devoted to making lasting contributions to the fields of human and machine intelligence.

Art

I create visual and audio reactive digital art with TouchDesigner where I aim for nothing more than achieving an aesthetic that is a satisfactory blend of psychedelia and technocore using mathematical ideas.

My writings have much more intellectual motivation. The genres span from philosophical essays to fictional short stories. Some topics and themes of interest are science fiction, horror, absurdism, avant-garde, postmodernism, and consciousness.
